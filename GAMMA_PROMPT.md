# Awaaz - Gamma Presentation Prompt

Create a **modern, professional, and visually sophisticated** presentation about **Awaaz** - a hyperlocal community intelligence platform for Indian rural and semi-urban communities. 

## Presentation Specifications

**Target Audience:** Social impact investors, NGO program leads, and government/municipal stakeholders in India

**Tone:** Warm, clear, community-focused, and confidently modern (American English)

**Output Language:** American English

**Amount of Text:** Keep text minimal and impactful. Use bullet points, short phrases, and visual hierarchy. Maximum 5-7 bullet points per slide, with concise headlines (under 10 words).

**Visual Style:** 
- **Modern, clean, and professional** - Think contemporary tech startup aesthetic, not traditional or old-fashioned
- **Color Palette:** Match the Awaaz frontend theme:
  - Primary: Deep slate/dark blue-gray (#1a1f2e or hsl(222, 47%, 11%))
  - Background: Clean white (#ffffff) with subtle light gray accents
  - Accents: Muted grays (#f5f5f5, #e5e5e5) for subtle depth
  - Chart/Data colors: Subtle blues, teals, and warm oranges for visual interest
  - Avoid: Bright, saturated colors or warm oranges/greens that feel dated
- **Typography:** Modern sans-serif (similar to Lato or Inter), clean and highly legible
- **Design Elements:** 
  - Minimal, geometric shapes
  - Subtle gradients or solid colors (no heavy textures)
  - Clean icons and illustrations (line art style, not hand-drawn)
  - Generous white space
  - Smooth, subtle animations
  - Card-based layouts with soft shadows
  - Rounded corners (0.5rem radius)
- **Visuals Should Feel:** Tech-forward, polished, trustworthy, accessible, and modern - like a contemporary SaaS product or modern civic tech platform

## Design Guidelines Summary

**DO:**
- ✅ Use modern, clean design (think Stripe, Linear, or modern civic tech)
- ✅ Minimal text, visual-first approach
- ✅ Deep slate/dark blue-gray primary color
- ✅ Clean white backgrounds with subtle grays
- ✅ Modern sans-serif typography
- ✅ Geometric, minimal icons
- ✅ Generous white space
- ✅ Subtle shadows and rounded corners
- ✅ Professional, tech-forward aesthetic

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Traditional or old-fashioned visuals

- ❌ Bright, saturated warm colors (oranges, greens)
- ❌ Cluttered layouts or busy designs
- ❌ Hand-drawn or sketchy illustrations
- ❌ Heavy textures or gradients
- ❌ Old-style charts or diagrams
- ❌ Too much text on slides
- ❌ **Important:** Traditional knowledge should be shown as modern and recent (can be from 2-3 months ago), not ancient or elderly-focused

**Overall Feel:** Modern SaaS product, contemporary civic tech platform, professional startup pitch deck - polished, trustworthy, and forward-thinking.
